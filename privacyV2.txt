Interactive Breathwork Privacy Policy (version 1.3.1 and above)

This app collects the following data:
- Crash logs: If the app crashes, we will collect logs data directly related to the crash. 
  Purpose: to identify the cause of the crash to fix the app.
- App activity: How long you use each feature of the app. 
  Purpose: to know what feature to improve.

This app collects data using the following third-party service providers:
- Google Analytics for Firebase (Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/policies/analytics)
- Firebase Crashlytics (Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

This app does not share collected data with third parties.
